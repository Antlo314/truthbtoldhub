PART E — BRAND DO'S AND DON'TS (for both tools)
================================================================================
DO:
  • Modern, night, soulful, clean
  • African American spiritual depth without pastiche
  • Warm lows, restrained highs
  • Short tails for SFX (web-friendly)

DON'T:
  • Stereotype "tribal" drums as default spiritual cue
  • Loud horror, jump scares
  • Copyrighted melodies (games themes, pop songs)
  • Overlong SFX (harder to mix in browser)
